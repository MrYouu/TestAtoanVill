About Us

Antoan Vill was established in 1998. The company’s main activity is the production of ladies’ garments. Our products include trousers, skirts, dresses, light jackets, blouses. Antoan Vill has its own Code of ethics, based on the Bulgarian laws, regulating labour, health and safety at work, the principles of social responsibility and sustainable development. The company has implemented System for quality management in accordance with international standard ISO 9001:2015 and successfully attained Amfori BSCI audit.
